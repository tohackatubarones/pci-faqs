Where an entity is being assessed for the first time against a PCI DSS requirement with a defined timeframe, it is considered an initial PCI DSS assessment for that requirement. This means the entity has never undergone a prior assessment to that requirement, where the assessment resulted in submission of a compliance validation document (for example, an AOC, SAQ, or ROC).<br/>
