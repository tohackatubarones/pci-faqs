Secure Software Assessors are qualified by PCI SSC to validate payment software adherence to the Secure Software Standard.<br/>
<br/>

Qualified Secure Software Assessors will have the <b>Assessment Type</b> of "Secure Software" noted in their listing in the <a href="https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/assessors_and_solutions/software_security_framework_assessors" rel="noopener" style="color: #0563c1;text-decoration: underline" target="_blank"><span style="color: #3498db">PCI SSC list of Software Security Framework Assessors</a><span style="color: #3498db">.<br/>
