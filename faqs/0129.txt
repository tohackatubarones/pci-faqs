The Prioritized Approach tool is intended to help guide non-compliant entities to work through the process of becoming PCI DSS compliant. The Prioritized Approach does not supersede or replace the PCI DSS; rather, it can help to identify the quickest path a non-compliant entity can take to reduce risk to cardholder data.   <br/><br/>The Prioritized Approach focuses on six security milestones to incrementally protect against the highest risk factors and escalating threats.
