Service providers cannot use SAQ eligibility criteria to determine applicability of PCI DSS requirements for assessments documented in a Report on Compliance. The only acceptable SAQ for service providers is <i>SAQ D for Service Providers</i>. All other SAQs are intended for merchant use only.<br/>Merchants with environments that fully meet all the eligibility criteria defined in a particular SAQ may use that SAQ as a reference to identify the applicable PCI DSS requirements for that environment.
