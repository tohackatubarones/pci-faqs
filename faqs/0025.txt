An inactive user account<span style="color: black"> is one that has not been used in over 90 days. Inactive accounts are often targets for attackers since they are generally not monitored, and changes to the accounts (such as a changed password) could easily go unnoticed.<br/><br/>Removing or disabling inactive accounts reduces the risk that they will be used to gain unauthorized access to the environment.<br/><br/><em>Note: The specific sub requirement number(s) and terminology may vary depending on the version of the standard being used.</em>
