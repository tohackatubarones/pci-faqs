An entity that receives and stores only truncated PAN does not need to consider this storage of cardholder data for the purposes of the SAQ eligibility criteria.<br/><br/>Merchants must meet all the defined eligibility criteria for a particular SAQ in order to use that SAQ.  Merchants should consult with their acquirer or the payment brands directly (as applicable) to determine which SAQ they should use.
