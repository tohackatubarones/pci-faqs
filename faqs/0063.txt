<span style="color: #333333">Whether the inclusion of UnionPay in PCI DSS documents impacts an entity's PCI DSS assessment is determined by the PCI SSC Participating Payment Brands (American Express, Discover, JCB International, Mastercard, UnionPay, and Visa). Each Participating Payment Brand currently has their own PCI compliance programs for the protection of their affiliated payment card account data. <span style="color: #333333">Entities should always contact their acquirer or the payment brands directly to determine their compliance reporting requirements, including any potential impacts to a PCI DSS assessment.
