Each payment brand determines their own compliance validation requirements, which may include specific requirements for companies comprised of multiple or separate entities.   Organizations should contact their acquirer (merchant bank) and/or the payment brands directly, as applicable, to determine how to validate their compliance.  Contact information for the payment brands is in FAQ #1142 <a data-lr="1" href="/faq/articles/Frequently_Asked_Question/How-do-I-contact-the-payment-card-brands" rel="noopener" target="_blank">How do I contact the payment card brands?</a>.
